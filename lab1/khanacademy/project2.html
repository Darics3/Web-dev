Chicken Sandwich

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredient Amount
Bread 2 slices
Chicken 100 g
Cheese 1 slice

Steps:

1. Cook the chicken.

2. Place chicken and cheese between bread slices.

3. Serve warm.

Vegetable Soup

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 3 people
Ingredient Amount
Potatoes 3
Carrot 1
Water 1 liter

Steps:

1. Cut vegetables.

2. Boil them in water.

3. Cook until soft.
